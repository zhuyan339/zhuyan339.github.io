Systematic Review & Meta-Analysis Research

Sexual Violence and Shame: A Meta-Analysis and Systematic Review

Research Contributor | ISRE2024 conference abstract submission stage

Contributed to an early-stage collaborative systematic review and meta-analysis examining associations between sexual violence experiences and shame-related outcomes. The conference abstract submission synthesized evidence across psychological and clinical research, including studies of child sexual abuse, adolescent/adult sexual assault, lifetime sexual violence, and multiple forms of shame.

This experience strengthened my training in evidence synthesis, psychological literature review, meta-analytic research workflows, and collaborative academic research. I present this work as research experience rather than as a publication because the final public article lists a revised author group.
